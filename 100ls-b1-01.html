Making Plans with a Friend

100Ls Shadowing Material · Lesson 01
📄 Full Script
Hey, are you free this weekend? I was thinking we could check out that new café downtown. They have really good coffee, and I heard the food is amazing too. We could go Saturday morning, grab some breakfast, and maybe walk around the area after. It's supposed to be nice weather. What do you think? If Saturday doesn't work, Sunday's fine with me too.
嘿，你这个周末有空吗？我在想我们可以去市中心那家新咖啡馆看看。他们的咖啡很棒，而且我听说食物也非常好。我们可以周六早上去，吃个早餐，然后在周边走走。天气应该不错。你觉得怎么样？如果周六不行，周日也完全没问题。

📚 Key Vocabulary
check out
/tʃek aʊt/ · 连读 → "checkout"
去看看，去试试（非正式）
We should check out that new restaurant sometime.
grab some breakfast
/ɡræb səm ˈbrekfəst/ · "some" 弱读 → /səm/
随便吃点早饭（口语，轻松随意）
Let's grab some coffee before we head out.
supposed to
/səˈpoʊzd tə/ · 弱读 → /s'posed tə/
应该，据说（表示预期或传言）
It's supposed to rain all week.
doesn't work
/ˈdʌznt wɜːrk/
不合适，不方便，行不通
If that time doesn't work, we can reschedule.
🔗 Connected Speech 连读解析
are you → "arya"
/ɑːr jə/
Linking R + Glide
"are" 尾音 /r/ 与 "you" 的滑音 /j/ 衔接，两词融合。
💡 Hey, arya free this weekend?
What do you → "whatcha"
/wʌt də jə/ → /ˈwʌtʃə/
Assimilation + Reduction
"do you" 缩减，t+y 同化变 /tʃ/，整体像 "whatcha"。
同类：Did you → "didja"，Would you → "wouldja"
💡 Whatcha think?
supposed to → "s'posed ta"
/səˈpoʊzd tə/
Function Word Reduction
非句末 "to" 几乎总弱读为 /tə/，"supposed" 首音节也常吞掉。
同类：going to → "gonna"，want to → "wanna"
💡 It's s'posed ta be nice weather.
check out → "checkout"
/tʃek aʊt/
Consonant–Vowel Linking
末尾辅音 /k/ 直接衔接后词元音 /aʊ/，听成一个词。
同类：pick up → "pickUp"，find out → "findOut"
💡 We could checkout that new café.
